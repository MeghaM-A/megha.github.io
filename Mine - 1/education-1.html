Journey of Techbee HCL

I began my journey with HCL's TechBee program on November 28, 2021. My training started with the CH_HY_LK_ND_NG_VJW_JFM22_TECHBEE_74 batch from January 13th to March 16th, 2022, followed by the TECHBEE_AMJ22_ERS_FS_JAVA_1 batch from April 12th to August 8th, 2022. After completing my batches, I moved on to an On-the-Job Training (OJT) period from September 18th, 2022, to January 13th, 2023. This culminated in becoming a full-time employee (FTE) on January 13th, 2023. Currently, I'm working on the EDTECH-P&S Del-Digital-Practice project, focusing on simulation and problem-solving.
